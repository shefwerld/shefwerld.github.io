So guys, we did it. We reached a quarter of a million subscribers... It has been 1 whole year since Shef Kerbi News Network started up, back in September 22, 2018. Despite various difficulties, we've held on and managed to properly establish ourselves to Dreem Lend and the rest of Planett Popstah.
